HIT Tracki is developed by focusing on multi-carrier shipment tracking in one place (Works with HITShipo integration). Currently it supports DHL Express, UPS, Fedex, Canada Post, Purolator.

== Description ==

[HIT Tracki - Multi-carrier Shipment Tracking](https://wordpress.org/plugins/hit-tracki-multi-carrier-shipment-tracking) plugin, integrate the [HITShipo](www.app.hitshipo.com/) for tracking both Domestic and International shipments. According to the destination, We are provide tracking information. It supports all Countries.

Annoyed of clicking button to tracking multi-carrier shipments. Here is a Hassle free solution for you, [HITShipo](www.app.hitshipo.com) is the tool with fully automated will reduce your time to track multi carrier shipments.
